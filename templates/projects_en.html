History background



In the summer of 2023, we gathered 5 high school students from different cities in Ukraine and offered them to develop their own projects. Each idea was aimed at rebuilding Ukraine in the aftermath of the war.

For 6 months, the participants worked on their projects under the guidance of mentors Nataliia Pashkovska and Andrii Holiutyak, as well as external experts. We managed to film the process in the format of a documentary, where the heroes share their impressions and plans.
On February 16, 2024, in Kyiv, the heroes presented their ideas to guests at a private pre-premiere screening. Afterwards, they embarked on a journey abroad to share the story of Ukraine with the world.
